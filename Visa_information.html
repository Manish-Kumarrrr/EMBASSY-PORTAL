  • Overview

    With effect from 13 December, 2021 (12:01 AM Eastern Standard Time), the outsourcing service provider – VFS Global – will start accepting all visa applications, including Emergency Visa applications strictly as per the emergency criteria set out on this page. The applicants can now obtain regular Entry Visas through VFS Global without submission of any emergency documentation or reasons for travel to India subject to other eligibility conditions.

    The Emergency Visa service is only for US passport holders of Indian origin and their family members required to visit India due to a genuine family emergency, such as critical illness or death of a family member. Documentary evidence will need to be provided.

    The non-emergency visa applicants can apply for a regular Entry Visa through VFS Global, without submission of any emergency documentation or reason for travel to India, subject to eligibility conditions. Processing of such applications will be as per normal procedures

    Emergency Service has a fee that the Embassy/Consulates charges to expedite the visa application process. Applicants have to apply for:

    • ‘Emergency - Entry Visa’ for 6 months Single/Multiple Entry

    Note:

    • Emergency Visa will be granted subject to submission of complete documentation and the Embassy/Consulate may ask for additional documentation in certain cases
    • Applicants are advised not to make any travel plans before issuance of visa.
    • Emergency Visa will be issued for a maximum six month’s duration. The duration of Visa granted by the Embassy will be at the sole discretion of the Embassy/Consulates.
    • The Embassy/Consulate will have the final authority for grant of emergency visa subject to eligibility & VFS Global and none of its staff has any role in the decision-making process.

    • Purposes To Apply for An Emergency Service with documentary evidence such as letter from the hospital/death certificate, etc.

      • Genuine family emergency such as critical illness or death of a family member with documentary evidence
      • Wedding of self

    • Non - Acceptable Purposes to Apply for an Emergency Service

      • Attending wedding of family and friends
      • Religious purposes
      • Family Reunion
      • Tourism purpose
